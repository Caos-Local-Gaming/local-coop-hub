Play PC Games in Local Co-Op (Split Screen)

This hub provides tools, guides and setups to play PC games in local multiplayer (split-screen) even if they don't support it natively.

Includes custom configurations using Nucleus Co-Op, virtual machines, scripts and performance tweaks to enable couch co-op gaming on a single PC.

Current setups include Diablo 2 local co-op (same PC), with more games being added such as Halo MCC, classic titles and modern PC games adapted for split-screen play.
